Corsair F27

Anacortes, WA

After a year of searching for an F27, we located one for sale, on Craigslist, in Anacortes, Washington. In May of 2021 we flew to Seattle, then drove the 1.5 hours North to make the purchase.

Long trip home

The boat had been hauled to Anacortes from Georgia a few months prior. We coordinated with a national hauler to have her hauled to Portland, ME.

Home waters

At home on her mooring in Casco Bay

Cruising Casco Bay

It doesn't take much wind to get Mojo moving but on this day we had to rely on the outboard to get us to Pott's Harbor. We were rewarded for our patience, during the long slog, with a great meal and even better sunset.
