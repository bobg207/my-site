Sailing

My fleet. Past and present.

#1 Paceship PY26

The Paceship PY 26 is a Canadian sailboat that was designed by John Deknatel, president of C. Raymond Hunt Assoc., and first built in 1972

#2 Southern Cross 31

The Southern Cross 31 is a no nonsense double-ender designed by Tom Gillmer, a professor of naval architecture at the US Naval Academy.

#3 Beneteau First 305

The First 305 is a 30’ cruiser-racer sailboat designed by Jean Berret (France), built between 1984 and 1989

Current: Corsair F27

The F-27 Sport Cruiser is an American trailerable trimaran sailboat that was designed by New Zealander Ian Farrier in 1984–1985.
